WHAT ANSH MEHRA HAS ACHIEVED SO FAR
  • Ansh is a Sr. Product Designer at Zuddl, which is a virtual events hosting platform. He is a filmmaker turned engineer who eventually pursued UX Design.
  • As a student of Engineering, he represented his college at some of the most prestigious hackathons in the world, including hackMIT 2018 (held at Massachusetts Institute of Technology) and HackHarvard 2018 (held at Harvard University).
  • Out of hundreds of teams from 17 countries, Ansh and his team were one of the 12 Finalists at the Microsoft Imagine Cup Asia Regional Finals 2019, held in Sydney, Australia.
  • After college, he interned at Swiggy as a UX Design Intern and eventually joined Zuddl as their first UX Designer. He was responsible for designing Zuddl's entire MVP from scratch. Zuddl has recently closed its Series A funding round of $13.5M in February 2022.
